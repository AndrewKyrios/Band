MAJOR LAZER

About

A conceptual EDM act based around a fictional character, Major Lazer was launched in 2008 as the digital reggae/dancehall project of Diplo, the globetrotting, tastemaking DJ/producer whose previous collaborations included work for M.I.A. and Santigold. Known as the hottest call-to DJ and producer, Diplo and Co. present an ecletic combination of different genres such as reggae, punk, pop and pure EDM to create a refreshing and insanely entertaining sound. Albums include: Guns Don't Kill People... Lazers Do, Free the Universe and Peace is the Mission.
